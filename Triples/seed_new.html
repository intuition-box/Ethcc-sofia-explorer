Step 1: Pin to IPFS Free — no gas

Upload metadata (names, descriptions) to IPFS via Intuition's GraphQL API. This does not create anything on-chain and costs nothing. Already-pinned items are skipped. Safe to run multiple times.

Step 2: Verify Review before on-chain

Important: Review every atom and triple before going on-chain. Uncheck anything you don't want to create. Items marked [SIDE] are side events. Once you're satisfied, proceed to Step 3.

Atoms = individual entities (sessions, speakers, tracks). Triples = relationships between atoms (e.g. "Speaker X → speaking at → Session Y").
